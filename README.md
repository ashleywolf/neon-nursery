# Neon Nursery

A Tamagotchi you raise with your face. Make the right expressions, earn accessories, watch it evolve.

**[Play it](https://ashleywolf.github.io/neon-nursery/)**

Adopt a neon creature and raise it by matching emotions -- ecstatic, heartbroken, furious, shocked, in love, sleepy. Hold the target expression long enough and your pet levels up. Earn 8 collectible accessories (Rainbow Bow, Flame Crown, Star Sunglasses, etc.) along the way.

Your pet evolves through stages based on how well you emote. It judges you silently.

## How it works

- MediaPipe FaceLandmarker for emotion recognition via facial landmarks
- 8 emotion challenges with meter-filling mechanic
- Pet evolution system (egg to hatchling to evolved form)
- Particle effects: hearts, sparkles, tears per emotion
- Emotion-specific procedural audio (happy chords, sad tones, angry noise)
- Free Play + Challenge + Party modes
- Single HTML file, no dependencies
